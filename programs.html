Explore the degrees offered by our department.

Programs

Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.)

Our B.Sc. program provides a strong foundation in computer science theory and practice. Students engage in a rigorous curriculum covering algorithms, data structures, software engineering, and artificial intelligence. The program culminates in a final-year capstone project, allowing students to apply their skills to solve real-world problems and innovate for the future.

Master of Science (M.Sc.)

The M.Sc. program is designed for students seeking to deepen their expertise. It offers specializations in advanced topics such as Cybersecurity, Data Science, and Robotics. The program combines advanced coursework with a significant research component, preparing graduates for high-level industry positions or doctoral studies.

CSE Department

Creating the next generation of innovators and leaders in the field of computer science and engineering through excellence in education and research leaded by our excellent faculty.
